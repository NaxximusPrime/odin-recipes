Lasagna



Fancy lasagna with a lot of meat!

This is the best Lasagna you will ever eat!

Ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Lasagna plates
  • Bolognese sauce
  • Cheese
  • more Cheese

Steps:

  1. You take a lot of meat and put it into a pan.
  2. You should than fry it until it isn't raw anymore.
  3. Put in some bolognese sauce and cook it up for about 1 min.
  4. After that is done, put some of it into a casserole dish and
  5. alternate it with lasagna plates.
  6. When the meat is empty put some creme fraiche ontop and bury it in cheese.
  7. I mean a LOT of CHEESE!
  8. Now you can add some more cheese and put it in the stove for about 20 min at 200 degrees Celsius.
  9. Time to eat now!
